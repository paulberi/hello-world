About fricket

The website is africket.com. Africket is an online market place that targets individuals and households. The objective is to encourage people and families to practice the concept of selling their belongings that they no longer find useful and which are still in good shape. This is in a bid the contribute to the environment and also to combat poverty both individually and as a community because the longer the useful lifespan of a commodity the lesser the pressure on the resources to replace them
